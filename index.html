2 Payment Flows

Stripe collects from companies. Rippling pays crew. SCN sits in the middle as the platform and employer of record.

Companies are charged via Stripe when crew are hired (card immediate for <7 days, ACH batch for >7 days). Escrow holds funds until job completion. This flow is identical for W-2 and 1099 jobs.

SCN → Crew (Payouts via Rippling)

1099 Contractor Payment

  1. Job completes, hours confirmed
  2. 7-day hold (existing escrow policy)
  3. SCN triggers contractor payment in Rippling
  4. Rippling sends full amount via direct deposit — no tax withholding
  5. Contractor receives pay in 2-3 business days
  6. Year-end: Rippling auto-generates 1099-NEC (if ≥$600)

W-2 Employee Payroll

  1. Job completes, hours confirmed
  2. 7-day hold (existing escrow policy)
  3. SCN runs off-cycle payroll in Rippling for that crew member
  4. Rippling calculates & withholds: federal income tax, state income tax, Social Security (6.2%), Medicare (1.45%)
  5. Crew receives net pay via direct deposit
  6. Rippling deposits employer taxes (FICA, FUTA, SUTA) to IRS/state agencies
  7. Year-end: Rippling auto-generates W-2

Rippling's advantage: Auto state registration

When a W-2 crew member works in a new state, Rippling automatically applies for employer tax accounts in that state. This eliminates one of the biggest headaches of multi-state employment.

6 Rippling Capabilities for SCN

Key Rippling features that align with SCN's event staffing model.

Unlimited Off-Cycle Payroll

Run payroll as often as needed — per event, per crew member — at no extra cost. SCN triggers a payroll run each time a job completes. No need to batch into biweekly cycles.

Custom Pay Types

Support hourly, flat/day rate, and bonus pay types. No fixed salary required. Crew are paid per-event for exactly the hours/days worked.

Automatic Tax Location Updates

When crew work events in different states, Rippling auto-updates tax withholding based on work location. Critical for touring events.

REST API

Rippling's API supports programmatic employee/contractor creation, payroll triggers, and data sync. SCN's backend can automate the full flow.

Zero-Pay Months

W-2 crew with no gigs in a given month stay active in the HRIS with no payroll obligation. Rippling maintains their tax setup for when they next work.

Integrated Support

4.8/5 on G2. Support team has visibility into HR + Payroll + IT setup simultaneously. Faster resolution than siloed systems.

7 Crew Experience Changes

1099 Contractor Crew

  1. Sign up on SCN → choose "Independent Contractor"
  2. Identity verification via Stripe Identity (gov ID scan)
  3. Rippling onboarding: SSN, bank account, tax info
  4. Browse 1099 jobs on marketplace
  5. Apply / get hired — same flow as today
  6. Complete job → hours confirmed
  7. Get paid → full amount via direct deposit (2-3 days)
  8. Year-end: 1099-NEC available in app (from Rippling)

W-2 Employee Crew

  1. Sign up on SCN → choose "W-2 Employee"
  2. Identity verification via Stripe Identity (gov ID scan)
  3. Rippling onboarding: SSN, bank account, W-4, I-9
  4. Browse W-2 jobs on marketplace
  5. Apply / get hired — same flow
  6. Complete job → hours confirmed
  7. Get paid → net pay via direct deposit (taxes withheld)
  8. Year-end: W-2 available in app (from Rippling)
  9. Pay stubs: Visible per event (gross, deductions, net)
Crew picks classification once at onboarding (locked after).

No per-job switching. A crew member is either W-2 or 1099 for all jobs. If they want to switch, they contact support. This simplifies Rippling's worker setup and avoids tax complexity.
