Capabilities:

  • Full control over payment method (can disable credit cards)

  • Built-in W-9 collection

  • Escrow-style fund holding
  • No domestic transfer fee

2️⃣ PayPal Marketplace (Commerce Platform)

Item PayPal (Card or Bank)

Payout Fee $0 (internal split)

  • Cannot control funding source (buyer chooses card/bank)

  • No built-in 1099 issuance
  • Marketplace approval required
  • Wallet-based dispute system

Executive Summary

  • Lowest Cost: Stripe ACH (0.8% capped at $5)
  • Best Control: Stripe Connect
  • Built-in Tax Compliance: Stripe (W-9 + 1099)
  • Higher Cost Option: PayPal (3.49% + $0.49)
  • Most Flexible Marketplace Model and the recommendation: Stripe

All fees shown based on standard US pricing. Actual negotiated rates may vary.
